## ?? OPEN ROLES

### 1. FLAIR BARTENDER (The Showman)
**Location:** Flavors Laganas (HQ)
**Role Type:** Full Time / Seasonal
**Salary:** Competitive + Tips

**The Job:**
You are the center of attention. You're not just pouring drinks; you're creating an experience. Speed, accuracy, and flair are essential. You'll be serving our signature frozen daiquiris and premium cocktails to a high-volume crowd.

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 2 years cocktail bartending experience.
- Flair bartending skills (fire flair is a huge plus).
- Ability to work under pressure in a fast-paced environment.
- English fluency is mandatory.

### 2. TICKET SELLER (The Hustler)
**Location:** The Strip & Beach
**Role Type:** Commission Based (High Earning Potential)
**Salary:** Uncapped Commission

**The Job:**
You are the face of No Limits Zante on the streets. Your goal is to sell tickets to our exclusive events (Boat Parties, White Party, etc.) and VIP packages. If you can sell ice to an Eskimo, we want you.

**Requirements:**
- Proven sales experience (promo, telesales, or face-to-face).
- Thick skin and resilience.
- High energy and competitive spirit.
- Social media savvy.

### 3. EXPERIENCE AGENT (The Concierge)
**Location:** Hybrid (Online & In-Resort)
**Role Type:** Commission Based
**Salary:** High Commission + Bonuses

**The Job:**
You are a holiday planner and VIP host. You'll manage groups (Hens, Stags, Birthdays), curate their itineraries, and ensure they have the time of their lives. You'll use our CRM to manage bookings and upsell premium experiences.

**Requirements:**
- Excellent organizational and communication skills.
- Experience in customer service or event planning.
- Ability to build relationships quickly.
- Tech-savvy (WhatsApp, CRM, Socials).

### 4. PR STAFF (The Party Starter)
**Location:** Outside Flavors Venues
**Role Type:** Seasonal
**Salary:** Base + Commission

**The Job:**
Get people IN the venue. You are the energy at the door. You'll be engaging with passers-by, explaining our concepts (Spin & Win, Daiquiri Menu), and driving footfall into the bar.

**Requirements:**
- Bubbly, loud, and magnetic personality.
- Confidence to approach strangers.
- Persuasive communication skills.

### 5. RESIDENT DJ (The Vibe Controller)
**Location:** Flavors Laganas
**Role Type:** Full Time / Seasonal
**Salary:** Competitive

**The Job:**
You control the energy of the night. From chill sunset vibes to peak-time party bangers. You must be versatile and able to read the crowd. Genres: R&B, House, Charts, Old School, Reggaeton.

**Requirements:**
- Proven club/bar DJ experience.
- Ability to mix multiple genres seamlessly.
- Microphone skills (hype the crowd).
- Must provide a demo mix.

### 6. LIVE MUSICIAN (The Talent)
**Location:** Flavors Tsilivi / Special Events
**Role Type:** Part Time / Gig Based
**Salary:** Per Set

**The Job:**
Saxophonists, Percussionists, Acoustic Singers. We want live elements to elevate our events.

**Requirements:**
- Professional standard musician.
- Ability to improvise with DJs.
- High energy performance style.

### 7. WAITER / WAITRESS (The Host)
**Location:** Flavors Laganas & Tsilivi
**Role Type:** Full Time
**Salary:** Competitive + Tips

**The Job:**
Fast, friendly, and efficient table service. You'll be managing VIP tables, taking orders via our digital system, and ensuring the venue stays clean and welcoming.

**Requirements:**
- Previous waiting experience.
- Great memory and attention to detail.
- Positive attitude and a smile that never fades.

### 8. JUNIOR STAFF (The Apprentice)
**Location:** Various
**Role Type:** Training / Internship
**Salary:** Training Wage

**The Job:**
New to the season life? We'll train you. You'll rotate through glass collecting, bar backing, and basic service roles to learn the ropes of the industry.

**Requirements:**
- Eager to learn.
- Hardworking and humble.
- 18+ years old.
