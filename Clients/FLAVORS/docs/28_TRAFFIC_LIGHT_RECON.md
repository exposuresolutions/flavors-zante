## 1. THE LANDSCAPE (What Others Are Doing)

### A. The "Old School" Approach (90% of Venues)
Most venues in Zante, Magaluf, and Ibiza still rely on analog methods:
*   **Mechanic:** Guests are given a neon sticker (Red/Amber/Green) or a glow stick at the door.
*   **Pros:** Simple, no tech required, instant visual recognition.
*   **Cons:** Stickers fall off, glow sticks break, no data capture, no "viral" moment, feels dated (2010 vibes).
*   **Verdict:** **Low Barrier, Low Engagement.**

### B. The "Dating App" Events (e.g., Thursday, Tinder IRL)
Pop-up events in London/NYC run by dating apps:
*   **Mechanic:** You must have the app installed to enter. Your profile is your "ticket".
*   **Feature:** "Near Me" features show who is in the room.
*   **Pros:** High tech, verifies singles.
*   **Cons:** Requires downloading a heavy app, creating a profile, and relies on good internet/GPS inside a dark club. Friction is too high for a holiday resort.
*   **Verdict:** **High Tech, High Friction.**

### C. The "Silent Disco" Model
*   **Mechanic:** Headphones with 3 channels (Red/Blue/Green LED lights).
*   **Relevance:** People choose their music channel, but the color signals their "vibe".
*   **Verdict:** **Visual, but not social enough for dating.**

## 2. THE "FLAVORS" OPPORTUNITY (How We Dominate)

We will bridge the gap between the "Old School" simplicity and "App" sophistication using **Web-Based Gamification (No App Download)**.

### The "Stop. Look. Hook Up." System

#### ? The Tech Edge (Web App)
Instead of stickers, the user's phone becomes the beacon.
1.  **QR Code Entry:** Scan a code at the table/bar.
2.  **Instant Status:** Tap Red, Amber, or Green.
3.  **The "Digital Pass":** The screen glows the chosen color (acting like a glow stick but brighter and cooler).
4.  **Gamified Missions:**
    *   *Green Mission:* "Find someone with a Blue Lagoon cocktail and ask for a sip."
    *   *Amber Mission:* "Flip a coin. Heads = Single, Tails = Taken."
    *   *Red Mission:* "Buy a round of shots for your crew."

#### ? The "Live Room" Vibe (Social Proof)
*   **Screens:** Display a live "Vibe Check" ticker on the venue TVs.
    *   *"Current Room Status: 60% SINGLE / 30% TAKEN / 10% LYING"*
    *   This drives FOMO and encourages people to join the game to influence the stats.

#### ? The "Icebreaker" (WhatsApp Integration)
*   **Problem:** Approaching someone is scary.
*   **Solution:** The Green Status on the app has a "Send Drink" button.
    *   It generates a pre-filled WhatsApp message: *"I saw your Green Light at Flavors... Can I buy you a drink?"*
    *   Users can show this to the bartender or send it to a friend to forward.
